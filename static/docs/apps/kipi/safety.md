# Child Safety Policy

**Last updated: March 3, 2026**

Kipi is committed to the safety and protection of children. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content and behavior on our platform.

---

## 1. Definition of CSAE

Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) includes, but is not limited to:

- **Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM):** Any visual depiction (image, video, or digitally generated content) of sexually explicit conduct involving a minor.
- **Grooming:** Behavior intended to build trust with a minor for the purpose of sexual abuse or exploitation.
- **Sextortion:** Coercing or blackmailing a minor using real or alleged sexual imagery.
- **Sex trafficking of minors:** Recruiting, harboring, or transporting a minor for the purpose of commercial sexual exploitation.
- **Sexualization of minors:** Any content that sexualizes or depicts minors in a sexually suggestive manner, even if not explicitly sexual.

---

## 2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on Kipi:

- Sharing, soliciting, or distributing CSAM or any sexual content involving minors
- Engaging in grooming behavior toward minors
- Soliciting sexual contact, images, or videos from minors
- Sharing content that sexualizes or exploits minors in any way
- Using rooms, comments, diary entries, or any feature to facilitate CSAE
- Attempting to circumvent safety measures designed to protect children

Violations are not limited to content posted publicly — private interactions, room activity, and any other use of Kipi's features are subject to this policy.

---

## 3. Reporting

If you encounter content or behavior that may constitute CSAE on Kipi, please report it immediately:

- **In-app:** Use the report feature available on any content or profile within the App.
- **Contact form:** [Submit a report](https://forms.gle/LYaCC9JNb77Ka5cc9)

All reports are reviewed promptly and treated with the utmost seriousness.

---

## 4. Enforcement

When we identify or receive a report of potential CSAE content or behavior, we will:

- **Immediately remove** the offending content
- **Disable the account** of the offending user
- **Preserve evidence** as required by applicable law
- **Permanently ban** users confirmed to have violated this policy

We do not provide prior notice before taking enforcement action on CSAE violations.

---

## 5. NCMEC and Law Enforcement Cooperation

We are committed to reporting confirmed CSAM to the **National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)** via the [CyberTipline](https://www.missingkids.org/gethelpnow/cybertipline), as required by U.S. federal law.

We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies investigating CSAE, including providing relevant data pursuant to valid legal process.

---

## 6. Child Safety Contact

For child safety concerns related to Kipi, including inquiries from Google Play, law enforcement, or external organizations:

**Contact Form:** [Submit a child safety inquiry](https://forms.gle/LYaCC9JNb77Ka5cc9)

This is the designated point of contact for all CSAE-related matters.
