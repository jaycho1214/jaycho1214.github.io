# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 15, 2026

## Overview

Nudge ("we," "our," or "the app") is a contextual planning application developed by Jaeyoung Cho. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Storage

Nudge is a **local-first application**. All your data — plans, categories, triggers, saved locations, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using SQLite. We do not operate any cloud servers or databases.

## Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your location on our servers
- We do not have access to your plans or notes
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Location Data

Nudge uses location services solely on your device to power location-based triggers (geofencing). Location data is processed locally and is never transmitted to any external server. You can disable location triggers at any time in your device settings.

## Notifications

Nudge sends local notifications to surface your plans at configured times. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on-device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

## In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. We never see or store your payment details. Purchase records are managed by the respective platform.

## Children's Privacy

Nudge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
